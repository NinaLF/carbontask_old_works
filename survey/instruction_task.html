In the following we will ask you to judge the carbon intensity of different carbon foorprints.
This means giving your best educated guess about how much carbon emissions (CO2) you think the presented carbon footprint produces .
You will be asked to make this judgment on a scale from 1 to 10, so you do not have to estimate any numbers.

On average a persons carbon footprint in xx is about xxxxx tons .
The carbon footprints you will see are reduced to 6 behaviors, so they do not equal a full carbon footprint of consumption a person produces.
In our task a very high carbon footprint based on the 6 behaviors would equal about 6 tons of CO2 and a small carbon footprint about 1 ton of CO2

Next you will see how the task will look like.
Then you will make 24 of these ratings.
Each time evaluating how much carbon emissions each carbon footprint produces
on a scale from 1 (very little) to 10 (very much)
